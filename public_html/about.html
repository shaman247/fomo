fomo.nyc is a community-built interactive map of upcoming events in the NYC area.

It works by visiting the websites of parks, museums, music venues, etc., identifying any upcoming events, and displaying them on a map.

My hope is that this becomes a useful resource for people to find events they are interested in and to engage with their local communities.

This project is a labor of love and will always be free and open-source.
